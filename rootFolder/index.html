About Us

Welcome to the American Heart Association at UTD! We are an organization that focuses on fundraising and volunteering for the American Heart Association Organization. We also specialize in educating club members on basic life support procedures and helping them receive CPR certification. We are currently doing an online format for meetings due to COVID. Navigate to our Events page to see our past and upcoming events. You can join our GroupMe in the Contact section and DM us for more information!
